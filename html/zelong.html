Zelong Li
  Zelong Li is an interdisciplinary artist, designer, and researcher who explores the intersection of technology and art. His research is focused on utilizing technology to uncover insights into the future through an understanding of the past. Zelong is particularly interested in the philosophical concepts surrounding the connection between time, space, and distance.

Zelong's work spans a wide range of technologies and mediums, including physical installations and in-browser experiences. Zelong's artistic practice is driven by a desire to push the boundaries of what is possible with technology, using it to create engaging and thought-provoking experiences that challenge our perceptions of the world around us.
