Statement
The research project focuses on the city of New York as our data pool, where we have gathered various datasets on a macro scale. We have gathered information on different aspects of the city, including population, transportation, construction, networking, and other relevant factors. Our objective is to reflect on this macro information and transform it into a skeuomorphic representation, creating an interactive model that visualizes this complex city in a unique and creative way.

While we have attempted to remain neutral and objective in our data collection, it is important to acknowledge that there may be some minor biases based on our individual perspectives. Therefore, we plan to carefully consider the data and represent it in a way that symbolizes the city as a whole, rather than any specific group or perspective. To achieve this goal, we have created a 3D model of New York City using Three.js and AFrame, which will be hosted online as an interactive living creature. Our project is not focused on addressing any specific issues, but rather on conveying our personal feelings and understanding of the city through an innovative and engaging visualization.

Overall, our project aims to provide a unique and creative perspective on New York City, using objective data to create an interactive representation that reflects the city's diverse and complex character.
